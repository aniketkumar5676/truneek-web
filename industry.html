BFSI

BFSI is a term that is used by industry experts.It denotes the companies that provide various financial products. BFSI comprises of insurance companies, commercial banks, non-banking financial companies, mutual funds among others. BFSI’s banking part includes core banking, private, retail and investment whereas financial services includes stock broking, mutual funds etc. This industry is growing at a rapid pace in India. This is due to compact regulation, acceptance, and development of technology in the country. Fin-tech companies have evolved their products and services to cover BFSI products. BFSI Industry has witnessed eye-catching reforms in the last 20 years and will remain a top industry for India’s economic development based on inclusive growth
